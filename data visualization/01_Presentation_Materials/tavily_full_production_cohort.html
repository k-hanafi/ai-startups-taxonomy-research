01. Landscape

The AI-Native Startup Landscape

In this full production cohort, 10.3% (4,554) of 44,386 startups are AI-native under this taxonomy — thin and thick LLM wrappers appear as classes 1C and 1D on the AI-native side. Most non-AI-native companies fall into traditional tech (0A) or non-tech (0C), with the 0B bucket capturing AI-augmented businesses.
